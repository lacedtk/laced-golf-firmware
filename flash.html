Laced Golf Sensor Update

Firmware v2.0 - LED & Haptic Enabled

Instructions:

1. Plug in one sensor via USB
Turn off all other sensors. Only connect one sensor at a time.
2. Click "Connect & Flash Sensor" below
Select the device with "USB JTAG" or "usbmodem" in the name (Ignore Bluetooth devices)
3. Click "Connect" then "Install"
Wait 2-3 minutes for the update to complete
Don't unplug during the update!
4. After each sensor, refresh this page
Turn off the sensor, unplug it, then press Command+R (Mac) or F5 (PC) to refresh the page before updating the next sensor

If you get an error:

Refresh the page (Command+R or F5) and try again
Important: Use Chrome, Edge, or Opera browser.
Safari and Firefox are not supported.
